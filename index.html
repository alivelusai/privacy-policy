Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

Thank you for choosing iBeatz. This Privacy Policy explains how iBeatz handles information when you use the application.

Information We Collect

iBeatz is designed with privacy in mind. The application does not collect, store, transmit, share, or sell any personal information.

The app does not collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Home or work address
  • Location information
  • Contacts
  • Messages
  • Photos or videos
  • Payment information
  • Advertising ID
  • Device identifiers
  • Browsing history
  • Audio recordings

Audio Processing

iBeatz uses Android's public audio APIs to apply equalizer effects to audio playing on your device. All audio processing is performed locally on your device. No audio is recorded, uploaded, transmitted, or stored on any server.

Permissions

The app requests only the permissions necessary for equalizer functionality and foreground service operation where required by Android. These permissions are used solely to provide the app's features and are never used to collect personal information.

Third-Party Services

iBeatz does not use third-party analytics services, advertising SDKs, tracking SDKs, or data collection services.

Data Sharing

Because iBeatz does not collect personal information, no user data is shared with third parties.

Children's Privacy

iBeatz is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13. No personal information is knowingly collected from children.

Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no personal information stored on our servers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any updates will be published on this page along with the revised effective date.
