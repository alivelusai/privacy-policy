Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Thank you for using iBeatz. This Privacy Policy explains how the application handles your information.

Information We Collect

iBeatz does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal information.

The app does not collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location
  • Contacts
  • Photos
  • Messages
  • Payment information
  • Device identifiers
  • Advertising ID

Audio Processing

iBeatz uses Android's public audio APIs to apply equalizer effects to audio played on your device. Audio is processed locally on your device only. No audio is recorded, uploaded, or shared with any server.

Permissions

The app only requests permissions required for equalizer functionality and foreground service operation where applicable. These permissions are used solely to provide the app's features.

Third-Party Services

iBeatz does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with anyone because no personal information is collected.

Children's Privacy

iBeatz is not directed toward children under the age of 13. No personal information is knowingly collected from children.

Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no user data stored on our servers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
